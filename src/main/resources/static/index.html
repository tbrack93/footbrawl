The Basics

  • Turn based strategy game

  • Teams gets 8 turns each half

  • Each player on a team can act on their turn

  • Run, dodge, throw, attack and more

  • Dice rolls, player skills and attributes sway outcomes

  • Most failed actions mean the end of your turn

  • Team with most touchdowns after 16 turns wins!
